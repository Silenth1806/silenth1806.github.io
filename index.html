Source based ios music player

Basically, I began to hate paying youtube an extra dollar every month for somehow a progressively shittier service. Then I thought "what if I just make my own app"
This app will allow users to download and install music "sources" online, as well as create their own to use with the app. I recently just got a apple laptop and have been living off of the dopamine from using swift/swiftUI. It's fun. I do want to end up releasing this on the app store, and in testflight soon. If it gets denied, I'll release it as a .ipa and open source it on github.
